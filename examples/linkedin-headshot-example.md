# LinkedIn Headshot Example

## User Input

`Create a LinkedIn headshot prompt for a consultant using this reference photo. I want it to feel trustworthy, warm, and premium without looking over-edited.`

## Skill Output Shape

### Recommended Visual Direction

Trustworthy + Approachable, because LinkedIn needs immediate warmth and clean thumbnail readability more than dramatic styling.

### Prompt 0 — Quick Baseline

Create a photorealistic professional LinkedIn headshot of the subject from the reference photo. Preserve the subject's exact likeness, including face shape, skin tone, age, hairline, hair texture, and any signature features. Trustworthy and approachable expression, slight natural smile, direct eye contact, blazer with open collar, classic centered head-and-shoulders composition, warm gray studio background. Window light camera left with soft shadow gradation and clear catchlights, 85mm compression, shallow depth of field, natural skin texture with visible pores, 4K output. Avoid plastic skin, uncanny eyes, distorted lapels, blur halos, color fringing, or AI corporate composite look.

### Prompt 1 — Base Generation

Create a photorealistic professional LinkedIn headshot of the subject from the reference image. Preserve the subject's exact likeness, including face shape, skin tone, age, hairline, hair texture, and signature facial features. The expression should feel confident but warm, with a slight natural smile as if mid-conversation. Dress the subject in a clean blazer with an open collar, framed as a centered head-and-shoulders portrait for LinkedIn readability. Use a large softbox 45 degrees camera left, soft fill at 3:1, hair light for separation, and sharp catchlights in both eyes. Use a warm gray seamless studio background, lighter behind the head and unobtrusive at thumbnail size. Capture with 85mm compression, shallow depth of field, subject sharp, natural skin texture with visible pores, 4K output. Avoid plastic skin, unnaturally uniform teeth, synthetic symmetry, uncanny eyes, distorted clothing, blur halos, color fringing, or an AI composite look.

### Prompt 2 — Lighting Refinement

Continue from previous generation, maintaining exact subject identity while adjusting only lighting. Reduce any flatness in the face and make the catchlights slightly sharper so the eyes read as alive at small thumbnail size. Keep the background subdued and the facial light warm but natural, not glossy.

### Prompt 3 — Expression and Detail

Continue from previous generation, maintaining exact subject identity while adjusting only expression and attire. Relax the mouth slightly into a more natural conversational smile and keep the blazer texture crisp and believable. Do not over-style the subject or idealize facial structure.

### Prompt 4 — Finish and Crop

Continue from previous generation, maintaining exact subject identity. Apply a subtle warm color grade, slight contrast increase, and keep skin tones natural. Generate only the 1:1 LinkedIn crop variant: face centered, eyes in the upper third, shoulders visible, chin with breathing room. No body parts cropped at joints.

## Why This Example Matters

This shows the most common use case for the skill:

- identity preserved from a reference photo
- tight LinkedIn crop
- warm but credible expression
- clear refinement path instead of a one-shot prompt only
